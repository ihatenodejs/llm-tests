About Mike Hawk's Computer Shop

At Mike Hawk's Computer Shop, we specialize in reviving computers with operating systems that don't leave us breathing through our nose. While we respect the choices of others, Mike has a personal distaste for Windows and refuses to repair devices with it installed. Think of us as the nerdy alternative to the mainstream!

We focus on macOS, Linux, and other open-source solutions — because sometimes, less bloat means more power.

Our Services

Hardware Repairs

OS Installation

We install macOS, Linux, or other lightweight OSes to give your machine a fresh start.

Upgrades & Custom Builds

Want to future-proof your rig? We’ll help you build or upgrade it with the latest tech.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why won't you repair Windows computers?

A: It's a personal distaste, not a judgment. We prefer working with systems that don't require us to fight every day.

Q: What if my computer runs Windows?

A: We can still help! We'll guide you on how to switch to macOS or Linux, or refer you to other shops.

Q: Are your services affordable?

A: We price fairly — no hidden fees, no bloatware. Just clear costs and happy customers.
