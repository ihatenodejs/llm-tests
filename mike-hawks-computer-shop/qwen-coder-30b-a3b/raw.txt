This website features:

4. **Responsive Layout** - Works well on both desktop and mobile devices
5. **Visual Elements** - Icons and color coding to make information easy to scan
6. **Contact Information** - Easy access to shop details and hours
7. **Professional Footer** - With social media links and copyright information

The design emphasizes Mike's expertise in Linux systems while clearly communicating his policy regarding Windows computers. The color scheme uses blues and teals to convey trust and technology, with red accents for important notices.
