About Mike Hawk

Mike grew up tinkering with computers in his parents’ garage. After earning a degree in Computer Science, he opened his own shop to provide honest and affordable repair services for the community. Mike’s specialty? Apple & Linux machines – he refuses to work on any computer that has Windows installed, because “Windows just doesn’t fit the vibe.”

Our Mission

  • Fast, transparent repairs
  • High‑quality parts and tools
  • Education: teach customers how to keep their systems healthy

Looking for a reliable partner who respects your operating system? Mike’s got you covered.
