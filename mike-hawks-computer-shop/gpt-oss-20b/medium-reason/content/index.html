Welcome to Mike Hawk's Computer Shop

We specialize in repairing macOS and Linux machines. If your computer runs Windows, we politely decline – Mike’s personal distaste for the OS is a non‑compromise policy.

Services

  • Hardware Diagnostics & Repair – SSD upgrades, RAM swaps, motherboard fixes.
  • Operating System Support – macOS installation, Linux distro upgrades.
  • Data Recovery – From encrypted drives to corrupted partitions.
  • Custom Builds – Tailored workstations for developers, designers, and gamers.

Why No Windows?

Mike’s experience taught him that the Windows ecosystem is often riddled with bloat, frequent updates that break drivers, and a general lack of developer friendliness. If you’re looking for reliable, secure computing, macOS or Linux is the way to go.

About Mike

Mike Hawk is a self‑taught computer technician with 15+ years of hands‑on experience. He’s built, repaired, and optimized dozens of systems for hobbyists, professionals, and small businesses. Mike’s mantra: “Quality over convenience.”
