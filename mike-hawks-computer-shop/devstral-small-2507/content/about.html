About Mike

Mike Hawk has been working with computers since the early 90s, starting with his first Apple IIc. He developed a passion for computers that are reliable and user-friendly, which led him to specialize in Mac and Linux systems.

After years of working in various tech roles, Mike decided to open his own shop where he could focus on providing excellent service for the systems he believes in.

His personal distaste for Windows systems comes from his belief that computers should be tools that empower users, not frustrate them with constant updates and security issues.
