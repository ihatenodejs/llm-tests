Our Services

At Mike Hawk's Computer Shop, we offer a wide range of repair services for Mac, Linux, and other non-Windows systems:

  • Hardware diagnostics and repair
  • Software installation and configuration
  • Data recovery services
  • Virus and malware removal (for non-Windows systems)
  • System upgrades and optimization
Important Note: Due to a personal distaste for the Windows operating system and its constant security issues, Mike Hawk's Computer Shop refuses to repair computers with Windows installed. We believe in providing the best service for systems that we know and trust.
