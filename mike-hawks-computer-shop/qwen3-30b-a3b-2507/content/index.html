Our Policy on Windows Systems

Mike Hawk has a personal philosophy that all computers should run on open, transparent operating systems. After years of dealing with Windows' frequent crashes, mysterious errors, and constant updates, Mike made a commitment: "If it runs Windows, it's not worth fixing."

We do not accept computers with Windows installed for repair. This policy ensures our focus remains on systems that truly deserve our expertise and attention.
